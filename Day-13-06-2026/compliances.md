### \#Core Frameworks \& Standards:

###### &#x20;   Depending on your industry and geographic region, you may be required to comply with one or more of the following:

###### &#x20;  

###### &#x20;  > SOC 2: Required for service organizations storing customer data in the cloud, verifying security, availability, and privacy.

###### &#x20;  

###### &#x20;  > GDPR: The European Union’s strict data privacy law governing how companies handle the personal data of EU residents.

###### 

###### &#x20;  > HIPAA: The U.S. standard for protecting sensitive patient health information in the healthcare sector.

###### &#x20;

###### &#x20;  > PCI DSS: A global standard for any organization that processes, stores, or transmits credit card information.

###### 

###### &#x20;  > ISO 27001: An internationally recognized standard providing a structured methodology for Information Security Management Systems (ISMS).

###### 

###### &#x20;  > NIST: Developed by the U.S. National Institute of Standards and Technology, a highly adopted voluntary framework detailing how to govern, 

###### &#x20;          identify, protect, detect, respond to, and recover from cybersecurity risks.
