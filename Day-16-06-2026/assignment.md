### &#x20; > Wireshark :

###### &#x20;                      Wireshark is a free, open-source network protocol analyzer. Often described as a "digital microscope" or "packet sniffer," it captures and translates raw, binary network traffic into a highly detailed, readable format. It is primarily used to troubleshoot networks, test software, and analyze cybersecurity threats.
