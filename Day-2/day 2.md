1. ### CIA triad:

##### &#x20;    > C - Confidentiality

##### &#x20;    > I - Integrity

##### &#x20;    > A - Availability

##### 

* #### Confidentiality:

##### &#x20;                   > Protecting sensitive information from unauthorized access.



* #### Integrity:

##### &#x20;             >  Ensuring data is accurate, consistent, and trustworthy.

##### 

* #### Availability:

##### &#x20;                 > Guaranteeing authorized users can access data when needed.







###### &#x20;Q)write three daily life example of CIA Triads ?



###### &#x20;sol)

|**CIA Principle**|**Example**|
|-|-|
|Confidentiality|Online exam, ATM pin, chats.|
|Integrity|Employee's report, online order details, Bank balance.|
|Availability|E-commerce site , Bank apps, electricity.|
